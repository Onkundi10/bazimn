Become a Seller

Bazimn empowers you to earn by offering your services to clients. Create your profile, showcase your skills and get paid securely.

Why sell on Bazimn?

  • Access a growing market of buyers seeking digital and local services.
  • Set your own prices and delivery times.
  • Secure payments held in escrow until the job is done.
  • Earn more with competitive commission rates.

Steps to get started

  1. Sign up & verify – Register with your email and phone, upload your ID and complete KYC.
  2. Create your gigs – Describe what you offer, set pricing packages and delivery times, and showcase your work with images or videos.
  3. Deliver great work – Accept orders, communicate with clients, deliver on time and earn more per project compared with traditional marketplaces.
  4. Get paid – After the client approves your work, funds are released to your balance. Withdraw via bank transfer, mobile money or PayPal.
