Dispute & Refund Policy

We understand that disputes may arise. This policy outlines how disputes and refund requests are handled on Bazimn.

Eligibility

Buyers may request a refund if the seller fails to deliver the agreed services, delivers defective work, or cancels without notice. Sellers may dispute refund claims if services were delivered as agreed.

Procedure

Disputes must be initiated through the platform within a reasonable time after delivery. Both parties can submit evidence. Our support team will review the information and decide the outcome.

Outcomes

Possible outcomes include a full refund to the buyer, a partial refund split between the buyer and seller, or release of all funds to the seller. Our decision is final unless escalated to arbitration or legal proceedings.

Chargebacks

If a buyer initiates a chargeback through their payment provider, seller funds related to that order will be frozen until the matter is resolved. Abuse of chargebacks may result in account suspension.

If you have questions about this policy or need help with a dispute, please contact support.
