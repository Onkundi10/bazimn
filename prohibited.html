Prohibited Services

To keep our community safe and compliant with laws, the following types of services are prohibited on Bazimn. We reserve the right to update this list and remove any listings that violate these rules.

  1. Illegal services – Any activity that violates applicable laws (e.g., sale of controlled substances, weapons, human trafficking). We will report illegal activities to authorities.
  2. Harmful services – Services that could cause physical or societal harm, including promoting dangerous goods, encouraging violence or exploitation.
  3. Fraudulent or deceptive services – Creating fake documents, hacking, phishing, facilitating scams or other dishonest behaviour.
  4. Financially risky services – Opening or managing financial accounts, providing loans, trading financial instruments, cryptocurrency mining or credit repair schemes.
  5. Violations of third‑party terms – Services that infringe on another platform’s terms of service or intellectual property rights.
  6. Misleading content – Deepfakes, fake reviews or any content intended to deceive or mislead.

If you come across a listing that violates these rules, please report it via the reporting tools on the platform.
